    • A fantasy curse-casting adventure. Make curses that can only be undone by true love's kiss!

    Curses, charms, potions - call them whatever you want.
    It's all just a channel for the magic in the end.

    TRUE LOVE'S CURSE is a fantasy curse-casting simulator and visual novel. Play as the owner of a magic shop and cast curses that can only be undone by true love's kiss.

    Figure out the right combination of words to cast a curse that matches your customer's needs! (Or don't - how you cast your curses is up to you!)

    Play time is <1 hour.

    FEATURES

    • A charming and silly story about a witch who sells any curse charm you can pay for
    • Unserious curse casting in a fantasy magic shop
    • Cute art and a spooky soundtrack
    • Unlockable in-game gallery and curse book!
